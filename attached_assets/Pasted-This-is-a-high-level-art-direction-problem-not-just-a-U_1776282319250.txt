# ⚠️ NOW — THE PROBLEM AREAS

---

# A. JOURNEYS / UNDERSTAND (ORB IMAGE)

## 1. Honest Assessment

❌ This is your weakest image

* Feels:

  * sci-fi
  * mystical
  * *chosen-one energy*

👉 Completely breaks your product identity

> Your app = “walk with God”
> This image = “cosmic destiny portal”

Mismatch.

---

## 2. Replacement Direction (VERY SPECIFIC)

You want:

> **Grounded movement, not spectacle**

### Best directions:

#### Option 1 (STRONGEST)

**A narrow path through tall grass at sunrise**

* Light just breaking through
* Slight haze
* No person or a very small distant figure

👉 Feels like:

* beginning
* uncertainty + peace
* invitation

---

#### Option 2

**A worn trail through woods with soft light ahead**

* Not dramatic
* Slightly imperfect path

👉 Feels like:

* “others have walked this”
* continuity

---

#### Option 3

**A person walking alone, seen from behind, small in frame**

* Not centered
* Not heroic

👉 Key:

* They are *on* the path, not posing

---

## 3. Copy / Layout Adjustment

Change:

❌ “Choose Your Path”
→ feels like decision pressure

✅ Replace with:

* “Start where you are”
* “Begin here”
* “There’s a path for this”

👉 This + grounded image = **instant alignment**

---

# B. DEVOTIONAL FALLBACK (RAISED HANDS)

## 1. Honest Assessment

❌ Kill it

* Most overused image in faith UX
* Feels:

  * performative
  * external
  * almost social-media worship aesthetic

👉 Completely opposite of “received”

---

## 2. Replacement Direction

You want:

> **Stillness + receptivity**

### Best directions:

#### Option 1 (STRONGEST)

**Light entering a quiet room through a window**

* Dust particles visible
* No person

👉 Feels like:

* something arriving
* presence entering

---

#### Option 2

**Person sitting quietly, head slightly down, natural posture**

* Not praying pose
* Not dramatic

👉 Feels like:

* listening, not speaking

---

#### Option 3

**Hands resting (not raised)**

* Open, relaxed
* Natural light

👉 Subtle but powerful:

* posture of receiving

---

## 3. Copy Adjustment

Lean into:

* “Receive today’s word”
* “Something for today”

Avoid:

* anything that implies effort or action

---

# D. GUIDANCE (MOUNTAIN IMAGE)

## 1. Honest Assessment

✔️ Concept is right
❌ Execution risk (external dependency + generic feel)

Mountains = good metaphor
But:

* Often overused
* Can feel “motivational poster” if wrong crop

---

## 2. Recommendation

👉 YES — replace with local asset

### Best directions:

#### Option 1 (STRONGEST)

**Wide open sky + horizon (minimal land)**

* Almost empty
* Quiet

👉 Feels:

* space to speak
* emotional openness

---

#### Option 2

**Foggy valley or soft rolling hills**

* Low contrast
* muted

👉 Feels:

* processing
* uncertainty held gently

---

#### Option 3

**Interior scene (very underrated option)**

**Empty chair across from another chair**

👉 This is powerful:

* implies conversation
* grounded
* not cliché spiritual imagery

---

## 3. Copy / Layout Adjustment

Guidance is your most human feature.

Make sure:

* Image does NOT overpower
* Text leads
* Input field feels like:

  > “you can say something here”

**subtle > obvious**
