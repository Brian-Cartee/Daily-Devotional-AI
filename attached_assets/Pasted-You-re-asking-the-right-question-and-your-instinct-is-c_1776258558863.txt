## **Guidance on Emotional Framing of Entry Screens**

---

### **First — Validate What’s Working**

The current screen is strong.

It does three things very well:

* It creates **immediate emotional entry**
* It lowers the barrier to expression
* It signals: *“you don’t have to have it together to be here”*

👉 That is a strength — do NOT lose that

---

### **But Your Instinct Is Also Right**

Right now, the emotional framing leans toward:

> “This is for when something is wrong”

Even though:

* many users will arrive in pain
* not all users will

---

### **The Real Risk (Subtle but Important)**

You don’t risk alienating hurting users…

👉 You risk quietly excluding:

* people at peace
* people in gratitude
* people just wanting to grow

---

## **Reframe the Goal**

Not:

> balance pain vs. positivity

But:

> **expand the doorway without weakening it**

---

## **Key Insight**

This screen is not about:

> diagnosing the user

It’s about:

> **giving them language to begin**

---

## **Recommendation (High Impact, Low Disruption)**

### **1. Keep the current emotional options (DO NOT REMOVE)**

* Peace
* Guidance
* Strength
* Grief

👉 These are strong anchors

---

### **2. Add one subtle but powerful expansion layer**

You already started this with:

👉 **“Gratitude — I want to give thanks”**

That is exactly the right move.

---

### **3. Adjust the framing line (this matters most)**

Current:

> “What’s on your heart right now?”

This is good — keep it.

---

But the subtext:

> “Your answer shapes your time with God today.”

This slightly implies:
👉 *you need something*

---

### **Refine to something more open:**

> “You can begin wherever you are.”

or

> “Whatever you’re carrying — or grateful for — you can begin here.”

---

👉 This single change broadens the emotional doorway **without changing the structure**

---

### **4. Add ONE non-emotional entry option (important)**

Right now everything is framed emotionally.

Add one option like:

👉 **“Just spend time”**
👉 **“Sit with today’s verse”**
👉 **“Begin quietly”**

---

This captures:

* stable users
* disciplined users
* curious users

### **5. “Bring me a word” is VERY important**

That button is your:
👉 **non-diagnostic, surrender-based entry**

Keep it. Possibly elevate it slightly.
