We are refining the “Journeys” (Bible reading paths) experience in Shepherd’s Path.

This is not:
a library of plans.

This is:
an invitation to walk through something with God.

-----------------------------------
CORE EXPERIENCE PRINCIPLE
-----------------------------------

This screen should feel like:

- “There’s a path here for me”
- “I can begin where I am”
- “I don’t have to choose perfectly”

NOT:

- “I need to pick the right program”
- “I’m committing to a plan”
- “I should evaluate options”

-----------------------------------
HERO SECTION
-----------------------------------

Current:
“Choose Your Path”
“Guided reading plans through the heart of Scripture”

Problem:
Too decision-oriented.
Too structured.

Refinement:

Option 1:
“Start where you are”

Subtext:
“There’s a path for this moment.”

Option 2:
“A path for where you are”

Subtext:
“You don’t have to figure it out — just begin.”

-----------------------------------
“LIFE SEASON” SECTION
-----------------------------------

Current:
“Navigate a Life Season”

Problem:
Sounds like a tool or feature.

Refinement:

“Start with what you’re walking through”

Subtext:
“Tell us what’s been on your heart — we’ll guide you.”

-----------------------------------

Card refinement:

Current:
“AI-crafted · 7 passages”

Refine tone to:

“7 passages, shaped for where you are”

-----------------------------------
SECTION HEADINGS (CRITICAL)
-----------------------------------

Current:
STARTING POINT  
FOUNDATIONS  
LIFE OF JESUS  
SEASONAL  
KNOW GOD DEEPER  
HEART & LIFE  

Problem:
These feel like categories.

Refinement:

Make them feel like movement:

-----------------------------------

Instead of “STARTING POINT”:
“Begin here”

-----------------------------------

Instead of “FOUNDATIONS”:
“Build something steady”

-----------------------------------

Instead of “LIFE OF JESUS”:
“Walk with Jesus”

-----------------------------------

Instead of “SEASONAL”:
“This season”

-----------------------------------

Instead of “KNOW GOD DEEPER”:
“Know Him more”

-----------------------------------

Instead of “HEART & LIFE”:
“What you’re carrying”

-----------------------------------

Key principle:
Each section should feel like:
a doorway, not a category

-----------------------------------

-----------------------------------

We want slightly more invitational tone.

-----------------------------------

Current:

“You don’t need to know anything to begin.”

-----------------------------------

-----------------------------------

-----------------------------------

Anxiety & Peace:

-----------------------------------

Speak to the person’s condition, not the content.

-----------------------------------

Meaning:
- smaller

-----------------------------------
OVERALL EXPERIENCE SHIFT
-----------------------------------

Right now:
Browse → Compare → Choose

We want:
Recognize → Feel → Begin

-----------------------------------

IMPORTANT:

User should not feel like:
“I need to pick the right one”

User should feel like:
“This one feels right”

-----------------------------------
GLOBAL TONE RULES
-----------------------------------

1. NO PROGRAM LANGUAGE
- no “plans”
- no “tracks”
- no “curriculum”

2. INVITATION OVER DECISION
- “start”
- “begin”
- “walk”

NOT:
- “choose”
- “select”
- “compare”

3. SPEAK TO THE PERSON
- their season
- their emotion
- their question

NOT:
- content summaries

-----------------------------------
FINAL STANDARD

Ask:

Does this feel like:
“I’m selecting something to complete”

Or:
“I’m stepping into something I need”

If it feels like selection → rewrite
If it feels like stepping into → keep
