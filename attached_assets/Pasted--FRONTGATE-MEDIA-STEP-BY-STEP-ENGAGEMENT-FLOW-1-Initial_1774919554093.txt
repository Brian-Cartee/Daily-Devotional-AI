🔑 FRONTGATE MEDIA — STEP-BY-STEP ENGAGEMENT FLOW
1. Initial Contact / Discovery Call
What they do:
Ask about your product (your app, audience, goals)
Try to understand your mission + monetization
Evaluate if you’re a fit for Christian audience campaigns
What YOU should do:
Be crystal clear:
“Faith-based app”
“Free version + future premium”
“Goal: acquire users at scale”
Position it as movement + tool, not just an app
👉 Your angle:
“Helping people find personalized scripture and healing through AI.”
2. Audience + Offer Alignment
What they analyze:
Who your audience is:
Gen Z?
Moms?
Men struggling with purpose?
What action you want:
App installs
Email/SMS capture
Paid upgrades later
What YOU decide:
Your PRIMARY target first
👉 My recommendation for you:
Start with: struggling / searching Christians + curious non-believers
Not too narrow yet — you need data
3. Campaign Strategy Proposal
They will come back with a plan like:
Influencer campaigns (their specialty)
Email/newsletter placements (they own big Christian lists)
Paid ads amplification
Content partnerships
This is where most people mess up.
👉 You need to ask:
What’s the expected CPA (cost per acquisition)?
What’s the expected install volume?
What platforms? (IG, TikTok, email, etc.)
4. Budget Discussion 💰
This is critical.
Typical structure:
Agency fee
Media spend (ads or influencer payments)
Possible minimums
👉 Reality:
You’re likely looking at:
$3K–$10K test campaign (entry level)
Can scale much higher
What YOU should do:
Say:
“I want to start with a controlled test to validate CPA before scaling.”
This keeps you in control.
5. Creative + Messaging Development
They will either:
Ask YOU for assets (videos, messaging)
Or help guide content creation
👉 For YOU:
Use your UGC creators (you already started this ✔️)
Focus messaging on:
Pain → solution → hope
Example:
“Feeling lost?”
“Find scripture tailored to your situation”
“Free. No pressure.”
6. Launch Campaign 🚀
They will:
Deploy influencers
Run email blasts
Possibly run ads
What matters here:
TRACKING
👉 You MUST have:
App install tracking
Source tracking (who drove installs)
Otherwise → wasted money
7. Performance Review + Optimization
After campaign:
They’ll report:
Installs
Click-through rate
Engagement
👉 YOU need to focus on:
Cost per user
Retention (are people actually using it?)
8. Scale or Walk Decision
This is the fork in the road:
If it works:
Double down
Increase spend
Expand influencer pool
If it doesn’t:
Adjust messaging OR
Walk away early
⚠️ REAL TALK (IMPORTANT)
FrontGate is strong in audience access, but:
They are not miracle workers
They don’t fix weak messaging or product gaps
They can burn budget fast if you’re not disciplined
🎯 HOW YOU SHOULD APPROACH THEM (STRATEGIC EDGE)
When you talk to them, position like this:
“I’m not looking for exposure. I’m looking for efficient user acquisition and long-term scaling.”
That instantly puts you in a different category than 90% of their clients.
🔥 YOUR SMART GAME PLAN
If I were you:
Phase 1 (NOW)
Build:
2–3 strong UGC videos
Clear app messaging
Soft launch organically
Phase 2 (WITH FRONTGATE)
Run small test campaign ($3K–$5K)
Measure CPA
Phase 3
If CPA is good → scale aggressively
If not → pivot BEFORE spending more
💡 FINAL INSIGHT
FrontGate is a distribution engine, not a strategy engine.
👉 Your success depends on:
Your messaging
Your funnel
Your clarity
