We are refining the Prayer Journal experience in Shepherd’s Path.

This is not a storage system.
This is not a feature hub.

This is:
a quiet place where someone returns to see how God has been moving in their life.

Every word, state, and interaction must reflect that.

-----------------------------------
CORE EXPERIENCE PRINCIPLE
-----------------------------------

The Journal should feel like:

- a record of a relationship
- a place of remembrance
- something personal, not mechanical
- something sacred, not functional

NOT:
- a dashboard
- a collection of entries
- a productivity tool

-----------------------------------
HEADER + POSITIONING
-----------------------------------

Current:
“Prayers, reflections, scriptures & sermon notes”

Problem:
This is a feature list.

Refinement:

Replace with something like:

“A place to remember what God is doing in your life.”

OR

“Where your moments with God are kept.”

Key principle:
Describe meaning, not contents.

-----------------------------------
EMPTY STATES (CRITICAL)
-----------------------------------

These are the FIRST experience for many users.

They currently feel like:
system messages

They must feel like:
gentle invitations

-----------------------------------

PRAYERS TAB

Replace:
“Your prayer journal is waiting.”

With:

“This is where your prayers will live.”

Subtext:
“When you're ready, bring something to God.”

CTA:
“Start with today’s moment”

-----------------------------------

REFLECTIONS TAB

Replace:
“No reflections saved yet.”

With:

“Your reflections will gather here.”

Subtext:
“Moments where something spoke to you… you’ll find them again.”

-----------------------------------

SCRIPTURES TAB

Replace:
“No scriptures saved yet.”

With:

“The words that stay with you will be here.”

Subtext:
“Save the ones you don’t want to forget.”

-----------------------------------

MEMORY TAB

Replace:
“No memory verses yet.”

With:

“What you carry with you will grow here.”

Subtext:
“Come back to what you’re learning by heart.”

-----------------------------------

SERMON NOTES TAB

Replace:
Empty/system tone

With:

“A place to hold what you heard.”

Subtext:
“So it doesn’t fade.”

-----------------------------------

Key principle:
Empty states should feel like:
“this will become something meaningful”

-----------------------------------
FIRST-TIME GUIDE / ONBOARDING MESSAGE
-----------------------------------

Current:
“Everything you do in the app flows here”

Problem:
Too product-oriented

Refinement:

Replace with:

“Over time, this becomes a record of your walk.”

OR

“You’ll begin to see patterns… prayers, answers, moments.”

Key principle:
No system explanation — only meaning.

-----------------------------------
SPIRITUAL LETTER (HIGH VALUE FEATURE)
-----------------------------------

This is one of your most powerful differentiators.

Current:
“You’ve been writing. There’s something to reflect on.”

Close, but still slightly notification-like.

Refinement:

“You’ve been showing up… there’s something here for you.”

Button:
“Read what’s been forming”

OR

“See what’s been growing”

Key principle:
This should feel:
deeply personal, not triggered

-----------------------------------
FLASHBACK / LOOKING BACK CARD
-----------------------------------

Already strong concept.

Refinement:

Instead of:
“Looking back”

Consider:

“Remember this?”

OR

“From a little while ago…”

Optional subtext:
“You were here — look at this now.”

Key principle:
Make it feel like rediscovery.

-----------------------------------
DELETE / REMOVE ACTIONS
-----------------------------------

Current:
“Remove?”

Problem:
Too abrupt for personal content

Refinement:

“Remove this entry?”

Subtext:
“You won’t be able to bring it back.”

Optional softer alternative:

“Let this go?”

Key principle:
Respect emotional weight of content.

-----------------------------------
EXPORT
-----------------------------------

Current:
Functional

Refinement (optional):

“Download your journal”

OR

“Keep a copy”

Key principle:
Even utility should feel human.

-----------------------------------
GLOBAL TONE RULES
-----------------------------------

1. NO SYSTEM LANGUAGE
- no “saved yet”
- no “data”
- no “flows here”

2. SPEAK IN MEANING
- memory
- growth
- reflection
- journey
- presence

3. QUIET CONFIDENCE
- no hype
- no push
- no urgency

4. CONSISTENCY
- same voice as devotional + guidance
- feels like one presence across the app

-----------------------------------
FINAL STANDARD

Ask of every line:

Does this feel like:
- a product explaining itself?

Or:
- a place someone would want to return to?

If it feels like a product, rewrite it.
If it feels like a place, keep it.

-----------------------------------

Do not redesign structure.
Refine language, tone, and emotional positioning.
