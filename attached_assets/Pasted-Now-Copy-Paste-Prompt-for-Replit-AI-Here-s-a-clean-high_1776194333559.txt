Prompt: Testimony Drawer / “Voices” Feature
Design and implement a sleek, modern UI component for a Christian faith app called “Voices.”
Concept:
A subtle, partially visible half-circle drawer/tab on the side or bottom of the screen that invites users to explore real user testimonies.
1. Visual Design
A half-circle or curved tab that slightly peeks into the screen
Should feel:
soft
minimal
slightly glowing or elevated
Include a subtle icon (e.g. speech bubble, ripple, or light symbol)
Label: “Voices” (or optionally no label, just icon)
2. Interaction Behavior
On tap or swipe:
Smoothly expands into a full or partial overlay
Animation should feel fluid and calming (not fast or mechanical)
3. Expanded State (Main Experience)
Display a scrollable stream of testimonies
Each testimony:
Short (1–3 sentences)
Emotionally authentic
Clean card design
Subtle attribution (first name or anonymous)
4. Motion / Feel
Gentle vertical scrolling
Optional slow auto-scroll (like a living stream)
Slight fade-in/out transitions between entries
Goal:
👉 Feels alive, not static
5. Emotional Tone
The experience should feel:
safe
human
communal
hopeful
NOT:
overwhelming
busy
social-media-like
6. Subtle Contribution Loop (Important)
At the end or between testimonials, include a soft CTA:
👉 “Share your story”
Should not feel pushy
Feels like an invitation, not a prompt
7. UX Goals
Spark curiosity when collapsed
Create emotional connection when opened
Encourage users to feel:
👉 “I’m not alone”
👉 “I want to be part of this”
8. Placement Options
Bottom center (preferred for subtlety)
Or right-side edge (like a handle)
9. Constraints
Must feel lightweight and fast
Should not block primary app experience
Easy to open and dismiss
10. Overall Goal
Create a feature that feels less like UI and more like:
👉 a living stream of shared faith and real human experience
🔥 Final Thought
If you build this right…
This becomes:
your emotional engine
your social loop
your retention driver
