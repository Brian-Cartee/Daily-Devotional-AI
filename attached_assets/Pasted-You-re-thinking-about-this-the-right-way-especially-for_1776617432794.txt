# 1) Free vs. Pro Split — Is It Fair?

### Short answer:

**Yes — this is one of the healthiest splits I’ve seen for a faith app.**
But there are 2 subtle risks you should address.

### What you got RIGHT

* Free tier is **actually usable daily** (this is rare)

* You did NOT paywall:

  * Bible access ✅
  * Devotional experience ✅
  * Core guidance ✅
    👉 This is critical. If you ever charge for “God access,” you lose trust instantly.

* Pro is **expansion, not restriction**

  * Unlimited AI
  * Depth (history, summaries, exports)
  * Continuity (streak protection)

That’s the correct model:

> Free = encounter
> Pro = continuity, depth, and consistency

---

### The 2 Risks

#### Risk #1: You might be slightly *too generous upfront*

Not fatal — but worth watching.

Free users get:

* Full devotional
* Full Bible
* Journeys
* Journal
* AI guidance (5/day)

👉 That’s already a complete product.

**What triggers upgrade then?**
Right now:

* Heavy AI users
* Organized / disciplined users (journals, streaks)
* Power users

That’s ~10–20% of users max.

---

### Fix (without taking anything away)

Don’t remove anything.

Instead:
👉 **Increase perceived value of Pro, not restrict Free**

Add:

* “Your week with God” (preview for free, full for Pro)
* Occasional “locked reflection insight” teaser
* Pro-only tone personalization (“gentle / direct / pastoral voice”)

---

#### Risk #2: Journals might be under-leveraged

Right now:

* Free = 1 journal
* Pro = multiple journals

That’s good, but not emotionally compelling enough.

👉 Journaling is one of your strongest conversion levers.

**Upgrade this positioning:**

* Free = “Your place to start”
* Pro = “Your life documented with God”

Add (Pro):

* Timeline view of growth
* “On this day” memory resurfacing
* AI pattern recognition (themes in prayers)

# 4) LocalStorage Pro Verification — Is It Safe?

### Short answer:

**No — it’s easily bypassable. But fixable without friction.**

Right now:

* User can open DevTools
* Set:

  ```js
  localStorage.setItem("sp_pro_verified", "true")
  localStorage.setItem("sp_pro_email", "anything")
  ```

👉 Boom — Pro unlocked

---

### Risk level:

* Casual users: fine
* Slightly technical users: trivial bypass

---

### Recommended Fix (important before Android launch)

#### Add lightweight server truth check

On app load:

* Call `/api/stripe/check-pro` OR `/api/referral/check-pro`
* Cache result locally
* Revalidate periodically (e.g., every 6–12 hours)

---

### Best hybrid model:

**LocalStorage = convenience**
**Server = authority**

Flow:

1. App loads
2. Check localStorage → fast UX
3. Quiet background verification → correct if needed

---

### Bonus protection:

* Sign responses with short-lived token (JWT)
* Expire every X hours

# 5) Google Play Billing — What to Double Check

You’re already ahead of most devs here.

* purchaseToken → Google API

#### 2. Acknowledgement step (CRITICAL)

---

#### 3. Subscription status checks

* Renewals
* Cancellations
* Grace periods

👉 You should periodically re-check status server-side

---

#### 4. Handling edge states

Test:

* User cancels subscription
* Payment fails
* Grace period
* Expired subscription

👉 Make sure Pro access:

* Continues during grace
* Stops cleanly after expiration
