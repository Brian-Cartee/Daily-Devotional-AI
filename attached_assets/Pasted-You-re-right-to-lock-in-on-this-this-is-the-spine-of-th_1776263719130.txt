# 🧭 THE FULL FORMULA (FINAL)

Every return moment should follow this structure:

---

## **1. Recognition — “I remember where you were”**

* Reference their last meaningful interaction
* No timestamps
* No mention of absence
* No sense of “you stopped”

👉 This should feel like continuity, not tracking

---

## **2. Re-entry — “Here’s a clear way back in”**

* Give **one obvious next step**
* Make it feel natural, not instructional
* No options overload
* No decision fatigue

👉 The user should not think — just step forward

---

## **3. Reassurance — “You’re not behind”**

* Remove pressure silently
* No catching up language
* No implied expectation

👉 This is the difference-maker

This layer is often just **one quiet sentence** — but it changes everything.

---

# 🔥 THE DEEP TRUTH

Without Layer 3, the experience feels like:

> “Pick up where you left off”

With Layer 3, it becomes:

> “You’re already where you need to be”

---

# ✍️ NOW — YOUR THREE SCENARIOS (READY TO USE)

These are written exactly how they should appear as **first cards**.

---

## 🟣 **1. Last action: Day 3 of a reading plan**

**Card:**

> You were moving through Day 3 of your reading.
>
> Day 4 is here when you're ready.
>
> There’s no need to catch up — just continue.

---

### Why this works:

* “moving through” feels active, not incomplete
* “when you’re ready” removes pressure
* “no need to catch up” neutralizes guilt

---

## 🟣 **2. Last action: Guidance session about anxiety**

**Card:**

> You were reflecting on anxiety.
>
> You can continue that conversation, or begin again from where you are today.
>
> You don’t have to carry it the same way.

---

### Why this works:

* Names the moment without over-specifying
* Gives a path without forcing continuation
* Ends with emotional relief, not instruction

---

## 🟣 **3. Last open: 10 days ago**

**Card:**

> You’re here now.
>
> You can begin with what’s on your heart today.
>
> Nothing has been lost.

---

### Why this works:

* No mention of time away
* No “welcome back”
* No guilt
* Pure presence

# ⚠️ WHAT TO AVOID (non-negotiable)

Never say:

* “It’s been a while”
* “You missed X days”
* “Let’s get you back on track”
* “Continue your progress”

Those break the entire tone instantly.
