## **Shepherd’s Path — Entry Flow & Navigation Guidance**

---

### **Question 1 — Entry Flow**

**Current risk:**
The current vertical scroll (verse → reflection → prayer → sermon) makes the experience feel like content consumption instead of a guided moment.

---

### **Recommendation: Shift to a Guided Entry Experience**

Move from:

* Showing everything upfront

To:

* Revealing each step progressively

---

### **Desired Flow:**

**Step 1 — Arrival**

* Show the verse only
* Calm, minimal
* One clear starting point (implicit or explicit)

---

**Step 2 — Reflection**

* Not pre-loaded
* Appears when the user begins

---

**Step 3 — Prayer → Understand → Sermon**

* Each step unfolds naturally
* Feels like progression, not a menu

---

### **Core Principle:**

> Don’t show the whole path — walk them through it.

---

### **Goal:**

* Preserve focus
* Deepen emotional engagement
* Avoid “scrolling through content” behavior

---

---

### **Question 2 — Navigation Depth**

**Current issue:**
Navigation is feature-based (Today, Explore, Journal), but users think in terms of intent:

* “I want to pray”
* “I want to read”
* “I need help”

---

### **Recommendation: Shift to Intent-Based Navigation**

---

### **Suggested Bottom Navigation (3–4 tabs):**

**1. For You**

* Daily devotional experience
* Guided entry point

---

**2. Talk It Through**

* Immediate help / prayer / guidance
* Emotional entry point

---

**3. Scripture**

* Bible reading
* Context
* Study / journeys

---

**4. Journal**

* Saved prayers
* Reflections
* Personal growth

---

### **Remove:**

* “Explore” as a concept (too vague and non-emotional)

---

### **Core Principle:**

> Navigation should reflect why the user came — not what the app offers.

---

### **User Intent Mapping:**

| User Need         | Destination     |
| ----------------- | --------------- |
| Start my day      | For You         |
| I’m struggling    | Talk It Through |
| I want to read    | Scripture       |
| I want to reflect | Journal         |

---

---

### **Final Summary**

**Entry Flow:**

* Move from scroll-based → guided progression
* Reveal steps instead of showing everything

**Navigation:**

* Move from feature-based → intent-based
* Make it instinctive, not learned

---

### **End Goal:**

* The app feels guided, not browsed
* The experience feels entered, not scrolled
* The user feels led, not left to figure it out
